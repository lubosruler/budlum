# Chapter 4: Networking and P2P Communication

A blockchain is distributed by nature. It has no central server. Instead, thousands of computers around the world, called nodes, connect to one another to form the network.

In this chapter, we will examine how Budlum nodes:
1.  Discover one another,
2.  Communicate with one another,
3.  Filter out malicious actors through peer management.

Budlum uses the industry-standard **libp2p** library as its networking foundation.
